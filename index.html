About me

I am an MBBS doctor from JIPMER with experience in both clinical medicine and AI-driven healthcare innovation. I am a Former Co-Founder and Head of R&D at Xpedicr, where I led the design of AI-augmented Telehealth platform tailored for the Indian healthcare context.

With strong operational insight, I specialize in bridging clinical needs with digital solutions, working at the intersection of patient care, medical data, and scalable technology. I bring a unique ability to ideate, implement, and manage healthtech projects that align with real-world hospital operations.
